RECIPE

1. Wash all the ingredients
2. Prepare garlic slices
3. Dice onion and tomatoes
4. Heat up a frying pan and pour some olive oil
5. Add the onion and garlic then stirfry until fragrant
6. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes
7. Add chicken or vegetable broth
8. Simmer for 15 minutes
9. Add salt and pepper to taste
10. Turn off the heat
11. Blend the soup using an immersion blender or blender.
12. Return the blended soup to the pot and add cream to taste.

Enjoy!
